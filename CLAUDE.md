\# CLAUDE.md - Capstone Project Option B: Data Analyst



\## Project Name and Goal

\- \*\*Project Name:\*\* Financial Wellness of Young Professionals in Latin America ("Datos que Hablan")

\- \*\*Goal:\*\* Transform raw survey data from 500 young professionals across 6 LatAm countries into actionable insights and an executive report to shape regional financial literacy programmes.



\## Environment \& Key Libraries

\- \*\*Python Version:\*\* 3.11+

\- \*\*Virtual Environment:\*\* venv/

\- \*\*Key Libraries Used:\*\* pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn



\## Naming Conventions

\- \*\*Scripts:\*\* Prefixed by phase number (00\_generate\_data.py, 01\_explore.py, 02\_clean.py, 03\_analyse.py, 04\_visualise.py)

\- \*\*Charts:\*\* Prefixed by order of creation in PNG format (01\_income\_by\_country.png, 02\_savings\_by\_age.png, 03\_satisfaction\_by\_ai\_usage.png, 04\_savings\_rate\_by\_industry.png, 05\_debt\_to\_income\_by\_country.png)



\## Data Files

\- \*\*Raw Data:\*\* data/latam\_finanzas\_2025.csv

\- \*\*Clean Data:\*\* data/latam\_finanzas\_clean.csv
